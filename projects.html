Smart Job Scheduler

A database-backed scheduler that prevents competing instances from executing the same claim while recovering work abandoned by failed workers.

REST API→Priority→Locked claim→Handler→Commit
Correctness

Pessimistic row locks, optimistic versions, claim tokens, and expiring leases protect ownership.

Transactions

Short REQUIRES_NEW boundaries isolate claiming and completion from handler execution.

Recovery

Bounded retries, exponential backoff, and lease takeover handle transient and abandoned work.

Verification

18 passing tests cover competing claimers, stale workers, retries, and transaction isolation.
